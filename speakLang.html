Speak in your Language

To be conducted by Community Welfare Cell

All of us have so much to express but sometimes we still feel like we haven't found a suitable medium to do so. Everyone has their own unique way of expression. With the exceeding amount of information around us and our individual perceptions, we feel the need to connect with people in a way we like.
The competition 'Speak in your language!' gives you this opportunity.
We present the following prompts based on which you can give your submissions, which you think describes your feelings or reaction to one of them. It can be a caricature or an animation, a song or a music piece, a dance video or poetry, any medium of expression you like.

Prompts:

1. Ab toh hum friend ho gaye hai naa? Masaan (2015)
2. Ice on fire (on the lines of global warming)
3. Tu meri beti nahi beta hai
4. Mard ko dard nahi hota
5. Pata hai, yahan se bahut door, galat aur sahi ke paar, ek maidan hai, main vahaan milunga tujhe, Rockstar (2011)
6. Ladkiyon ko car banane me nhi car me baithne me interest hota hai.
7. "All animals are equal, but some animals are more equal than others." Animal Farm, George Orwell

Rules:

* Participants will have to submit their entries as any form of art to express/convey any one of the themes/prompts provided by us.
* For video submissions, the upper limit of length would be 2 minutes.
* You can submit your entries starting from now till 26 June 2021.
* We will start publishing as you submit your entries on our social media handles until 26 June.
* The reactions and shares will be judged as on 11:59 p.m. 27 June 2021. Audience response will be the criteria for judgement.
* CWC pages at Facebook and Instagram will be used for judging.
* Winners will be rewarded with attractive prizes waiting for you this side.
